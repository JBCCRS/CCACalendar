Standing rhythms

  • Monthly principal newsletter (first week of each month)
  • Yearbook committee: half-days at the close of Q1, Q2, Q3 (sourcing photos for the yearbook and the year-end slideshow)
  • Birthday coordinator / classroom mom: keep celebrations consistent and fair across every child
  • Volunteer photographer: hand off the full event calendar early so dates are blocked
  • Community service project: one focus per grade, three touchpoints (September intro, January or mid-year, May finale)
  • Standardized testing twice (fall week of Oct 5, spring week of May 17), grades 3 to 6
  • Goal of one field trip or special event every month
